Once upon a time, in a wealthy and beautiful kingdom, there lived the Fairy of Mindful Consumption.
She was a radiant and wise guardian whose task was to protect the balance of the land,
ensuring that its treasures were used with care and gratitude.
She whispered her counsel to the King, guiding him to rule wisely and tend to the kingdom’s gifts.
Under her watchful eye, the fields flourished, and the people lived in peace and plenty.

One morning, the grand doors of the King’s hall burst open.
A trembling messenger rushed in, her face pale with fear.
“Your Majesty! Grave news! The Fairy of Mindful Consumption has been taken!
Three wicked witches have stolen her away!”.
The King’s heart sank, and a heavy silence fell over the throne room.
