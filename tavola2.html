In the kingdom, there had always been three witches:
the Witch of Mushrooms, the Witch of Molds, and the Witch of Worms.
They had long plotted to steal the fairy’s magic for their own gain.
Using their dark powers, they trapped her in a cage, sealing away her freedom.

With the fairy imprisoned, the kingdom began to decay.
The land withered, the air grew heavy, and the skies darkened.
From the shadows, an old woman watched,
deeply concerned for the future of the kingdom:
She knew that if nothing was done, the once-beautiful realm
would fall into ruin, consumed by the witches' greed.
