Without the fairy, the kingdom sank into chaos.
The king, once wise and fair, became consumed by his insatiable hunger.
Mountains of food piled up, wasted and untouched,
while the streets filled with trash and decay.
The once-thriving kingdom now reeked of neglect,
its people living in misery as the land crumbled around them.

The old woman sought out Perina, a brave and determined girl.
She approached her with urgency, her voice trembling with fear.
“The fairy has been taken by the witches
and is hidden in the woods!” she whispered.

Perina listened and without hesitation, made her decision.
She would not allow the kingdom to fall to ruin.
With courage and resolve, she set off on a perilous journey
to rescue the fairy and restore balance to the land.
